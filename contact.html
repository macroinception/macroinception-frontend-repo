Before You Reach Out

Quick answers to common questions.

What information should I include?

Help us understand your project by sharing: your technical requirements, timeline, budget range (if known), and any existing infrastructure or constraints.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer a free 30-minute initial consultation to discuss your needs and determine if we're a good fit.

What's your typical project timeline?

Projects typically range from 6-20 weeks depending on complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely. We work with organizations of all sizes, from early-stage startups to Fortune 500 companies.

Can you sign an NDA?

Yes, we're happy to sign NDAs before discussing your project details. Just let us know in your message.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

No problem! Our consultation includes needs assessment and recommendations. We'll help you identify the right approach.
