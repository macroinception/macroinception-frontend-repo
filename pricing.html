Frequently Asked Questions

How do you estimate project costs?

We provide detailed estimates after an initial consultation where we understand your requirements, existing infrastructure, and performance goals. Our estimates include the total number of hours required to spend upon development, testing, deployment, and documentation.

What payment terms do you offer?

For project-based work, we typically split payments into milestones (e.g., 30% upfront, 40% at halfway, 30% on completion). Retainers are billed monthly. We're flexible and can discuss terms that work for your organization.

Do you offer discounts for longer engagements?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for extended retainer agreements (6+ months) and multi-project. Contact us to discuss custom pricing.

What if the scope changes mid-project?

We understand that requirements evolve. For project-based work, we use a formal change request process with transparent pricing. For retainers, scope flexibility is built in.

Do you provide maintenance after delivery?

We offer extended maintenance contracts for ongoing support, monitoring, and optimization.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely. We often work alongside client teams, providing specialized expertise while ensuring knowledge transfer. We adapt to your development processes and tools.
